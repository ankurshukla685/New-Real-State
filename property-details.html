Daily Apartment

A daily apartment is a type of short-term rental property in the real estate market where a fully furnished apartment is rented to guests on a daily basis instead of a monthly or yearly lease. These apartments are designed to provide temporary accommodation for travelers, tourists, business professionals, or anyone who needs a place to stay for a short period of time. Unlike traditional rental apartments that require long-term contracts, daily apartments offer flexible stays, allowing people to rent the property for one day, a few days, or sometimes a few weeks depending on their need

In the real estate industry, daily apartments have become popular because they provide an additional source of income for property owners. Owners can rent their apartments to different guests frequently and may earn more profit compared to long-term rentals, depending on the location and demand.
